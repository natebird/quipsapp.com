Quote Style & Share Studio

Make your quotes pop with vibrant color, then share them your way. A redesigned Share Studio gives you full control over how every quote looks.

  • Quote Style — Quotes now show in vibrant color by default. Switch between Bold and Subtle looks, recolor the text, and choose a typeface
  • Share Studio — Customize a quote image before sharing — six card styles, square/portrait/story/wide sizes, light/dark/saturated backgrounds, and toggles for the author, date, and source (premium can remove the watermark)
  • Source Types — Note where a quote came from — book, movie, podcast, speech, and more — each shown with a matching icon
  • Copy Any Quote — Press and hold a quote to copy it, along with its author and date
  • More Personalization — New collection icons and colors, plus a new Salmon app icon (premium)
