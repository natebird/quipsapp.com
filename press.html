About Quips

One-liner

Quips is an iOS app for saving the quotes that move you and turning them into beautiful, shareable images.

Short (~60 words)

Quips helps people save the quotes that move them and turn them into beautiful images with Share Studio. Browse 80+ curated collections to start a library without a blank page, or build your own from books, podcasts, and conversations. Add a widget or Daily Quote notification to keep favorites close. No account required — quotes sync privately via iCloud.

Long

Quips helps people save the quotes that move them, style them into something worth sharing, and keep a private, synced library for themselves. Save a quote from a book, movie, podcast, or conversation the moment it lands, tag its source, and organize everything by author and tag. Browse 80+ curated Public Collections — Stoicism, literature, film, music, comedy, history, and more — to start a library without a blank page. Turn any quote into a beautiful image with Share Studio: six card styles, four sizes (including a 9:16 Story format), and full color and typeface customization. Opt in to a Daily Quote notification, or add a Home Screen widget that surfaces a quote and shares it with one tap.

Quips is free to start (save up to 25 quotes), with a Premium subscription ($0.99/month or $8.99/year) unlocking unlimited quotes, all Quote Style options, and watermark-free sharing.

About the Developer

Quips is built by Nate Bird, an independent iOS developer. The app grew out of a simple, personal problem: collecting quotes from books, podcasts, and conversations, and actually being able to find and share them again later — rather than losing them in a Notes app.
