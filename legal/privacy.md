## Introduction

Tweeting Birds ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Quips mobile application ("App") and when you visit our website at quipsapp.com ("Website").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use Quips, you may provide the following information:

- **Quotes and Content:** Text content of quotes, notes, and sources you create
- **Authors:** Names and information about quote authors you add
- **Tags:** Tags you create to organize your quotes
- **App Settings:** Your preferences for app appearance, themes, and colors

### Automatically Collected Information

The App does not collect any personal information automatically. However, the following technical information may be processed:

- **iCloud Data:** If you enable iCloud sync, your quote data is synced to your personal iCloud account (managed by Apple, not us)
- **Purchase Information:** If you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed by Apple through the App Store (we do not have access to your payment information)

### Information We Do NOT Collect (In the App)

Within the Quips app itself, we want to be clear about what we don't collect:

- The App does not collect your name, email address, or contact information
- The App does not track your location
- The App does not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
- The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services
- We do not sell your data to third parties

The optional Daily Quote feature uses local notifications that are scheduled entirely on your device. If you turn it on, the App picks a quote from your own library and asks iOS to show it at your chosen time — no data leaves your device, and we never know whether you use the feature.

Our website is different: if you choose to sign up for our newsletter or free email course, we do collect the email address (and optionally the name) you provide, in order to send you those emails, and the website uses cookieless aggregate analytics. See [Website](#website-newsletter) below for details.

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

1. **Local Storage:** All quotes, authors, and tags are stored locally on your device
2. **iCloud Sync:** Your data is synced across your devices through your personal iCloud account
3. **App Functionality:** Your settings and preferences are used to customize your experience
4. **Purchase Management:** Subscription status is managed through Apple's StoreKit to provide premium features

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All your quotes and data are stored locally on your device
- We cannot access your device data
- Data persists on your device even when offline

### iCloud Storage

- Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- We use Apple's CloudKit framework with automatic encryption
- Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We cannot access your iCloud data

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- Data encryption using Apple's built-in security features
- Secure communication protocols for in-app purchases
- No third-party data sharing
- Regular security updates

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control

- **View Your Data:** All your quotes are visible within the App
- **Edit Your Data:** Modify or update quotes, authors, and tags at any time
- **Delete Your Data:** Delete individual quotes or all data from the App
- **Export Your Data:** Export your quotes to share or backup

### iCloud Sync Control

- Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time from your device settings
- Disable iCloud sync in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Quips
- Deleting the App will not delete your iCloud data; you must manage iCloud data separately

### Subscription Management

- Manage your subscription through the App Store
- View, change, or cancel subscriptions in App Settings > Subscription
- Subscription data is managed by Apple; we do not store payment information

## Children's Privacy

Quips is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Website {#website-newsletter}

This section covers quipsapp.com specifically, which is separate from the Quips app described above.

### Newsletter and Email Course Signups

If you sign up for the Quote Unquote newsletter or the free 7-day email course on our website, we collect the information you provide in that form — typically your email address, and optionally your first name. This information is sent to and processed by **MailerLite**, our email delivery provider, who sends the emails on our behalf and acts as our data processor for this purpose. See [MailerLite's Privacy Policy](https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy) for details on how they handle this data.

We use this information only to send you the newsletter, course emails, and occasional related updates. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. We do not sell this information or share it with anyone other than MailerLite for the purpose of sending these emails.

Nothing is sent to MailerLite unless you actually submit a signup form. Our pages do not load MailerLite scripts or tracking pixels — simply visiting a page with a signup form on it sends nothing to MailerLite.

### Web Analytics

Our website uses **Cloudflare Web Analytics** to count page views so we can see which pages people find useful. It is a privacy-first analytics service: it sets no cookies, does not fingerprint your browser for advertising, does not track you across other sites, and gives us only aggregate numbers (like total visits per page). It cannot identify you as an individual, and we couldn't use it to do so even if we wanted to. See [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) for details.

### Fonts

Our website loads the Inter typeface from **Google Fonts**, served from Google's servers. Like any web request, fetching the font discloses standard request information to Google — such as your IP address and browser user agent. Google states that the Fonts service does not use cookies. See [Google Fonts privacy information](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy) for details.

### Data the App Requests From Our Website

The Quips app fetches public content — such as curated Collections, in-app announcements, and the text of these legal documents — from quipsapp.com and data.quipsapp.com. These requests are served by GitHub Pages and Cloudflare's content delivery network. Like any web request, they generate standard server request metadata (such as IP address and timestamp), which GitHub and Cloudflare log as part of operating their infrastructure. We do not access, use, or combine this metadata to identify or track individual users.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- **Right to Know:** We have described all data we collect in this policy
- **Right to Delete:** You can delete all your app data by deleting the App and managing your iCloud data; for newsletter data, unsubscribe or email us and we will delete it
- **Right to Opt-Out:** We do not sell personal information
- **Non-Discrimination:** We do not discriminate based on privacy choices

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on your consent and for contract performance
- **Data Portability:** You can export your data
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete all your app data yourself; for newsletter data, unsubscribe or email us and we will delete it
- **Data Controller:** Tweeting Birds is the data controller for your App data and newsletter signups; Apple is the controller for iCloud data; MailerLite processes newsletter data on our behalf
