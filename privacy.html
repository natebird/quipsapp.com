Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025 | Last Updated: July 6, 2026

In simple terms:

  • Your quotes stay on your device
  • Your data is backed up to your personal iCloud account
  • The Quips app doesn't collect data or track you, and doesn't use ads or analytics
  • Daily Quote notifications are scheduled on your device — nothing is sent anywhere
  • Our website sends your email to MailerLite only if you choose to sign up for our newsletter or email course (see Website)
  • Our website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics — cookieless, aggregate page counts that can't identify you
  • We don't sell your data
  • You control your data completely
  • Apple handles payments securely
  • You can delete everything at anytime

Questions? Email us at feedback@quipsapp.com

Introduction

Tweeting Birds ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Quips mobile application ("App") and when you visit our website at quipsapp.com ("Website").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Quips, you may provide the following information:

  • Quotes and Content: Text content of quotes, notes, and sources you create
  • Authors: Names and information about quote authors you add
  • Tags: Tags you create to organize your quotes
  • App Settings: Your preferences for app appearance, themes, and colors

Automatically Collected Information

The App does not collect any personal information automatically. However, the following technical information may be processed:

  • iCloud Data: If you enable iCloud sync, your quote data is synced to your personal iCloud account (managed by Apple, not us)
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed by Apple through the App Store (we do not have access to your payment information)

Information We Do NOT Collect (In the App)

Within the Quips app itself, we want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • The App does not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • The App does not track your location
  • The App does not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

The optional Daily Quote feature uses local notifications that are scheduled entirely on your device. If you turn it on, the App picks a quote from your own library and asks iOS to show it at your chosen time — no data leaves your device, and we never know whether you use the feature.

Our website is different: if you choose to sign up for our newsletter or free email course, we do collect the email address (and optionally the name) you provide, in order to send you those emails, and the website uses cookieless aggregate analytics. See Website below for details.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

  1. Local Storage: All quotes, authors, and tags are stored locally on your device
  2. iCloud Sync: Your data is synced across your devices through your personal iCloud account
  3. App Functionality: Your settings and preferences are used to customize your experience
  4. Purchase Management: Subscription status is managed through Apple's StoreKit to provide premium features

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your quotes and data are stored locally on your device
  • We cannot access your device data
  • Data persists on your device even when offline

iCloud Storage

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We use Apple's CloudKit framework with automatic encryption
  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We cannot access your iCloud data

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data encryption using Apple's built-in security features
  • Secure communication protocols for in-app purchases
  • No third-party data sharing
  • Regular security updates

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your quotes are visible within the App
  • Edit Your Data: Modify or update quotes, authors, and tags at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Delete individual quotes or all data from the App
  • Export Your Data: Export your quotes to share or backup

iCloud Sync Control

  • Enable or disable iCloud sync at any time from your device settings
  • Disable iCloud sync in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Quips
  • Deleting the App will not delete your iCloud data; you must manage iCloud data separately

Subscription Management

  • Manage your subscription through the App Store
  • View, change, or cancel subscriptions in App Settings > Subscription
  • Subscription data is managed by Apple; we do not store payment information

Children's Privacy

Quips is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Website

This section covers quipsapp.com specifically, which is separate from the Quips app described above.

Newsletter and Email Course Signups

If you sign up for the Quote Unquote newsletter or the free 7-day email course on our website, we collect the information you provide in that form — typically your email address, and optionally your first name. This information is sent to and processed by MailerLite, our email delivery provider, who sends the emails on our behalf and acts as our data processor for this purpose. See MailerLite's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle this data.

We use this information only to send you the newsletter, course emails, and occasional related updates. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. We do not sell this information or share it with anyone other than MailerLite for the purpose of sending these emails.

Nothing is sent to MailerLite unless you actually submit a signup form. Our pages do not load MailerLite scripts or tracking pixels — simply visiting a page with a signup form on it sends nothing to MailerLite.

Web Analytics

Our website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to count page views so we can see which pages people find useful. It is a privacy-first analytics service: it sets no cookies, does not fingerprint your browser for advertising, does not track you across other sites, and gives us only aggregate numbers (like total visits per page). It cannot identify you as an individual, and we couldn't use it to do so even if we wanted to. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

Fonts

Our website loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts, served from Google's servers. Like any web request, fetching the font discloses standard request information to Google — such as your IP address and browser user agent. Google states that the Fonts service does not use cookies. See Google Fonts privacy information for details.

Data the App Requests From Our Website

The Quips app fetches public content — such as curated Collections and in-app announcements — from quipsapp.com and data.quipsapp.com. These requests are served by GitHub Pages and Cloudflare's content delivery network. Like any web request, they generate standard server request metadata (such as IP address and timestamp), which GitHub and Cloudflare log as part of operating their infrastructure. We do not access, use, or combine this metadata to identify or track individual users.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

We use the following Apple services:

  1. App Store / StoreKit: For in-app purchases and subscriptions (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  2. iCloud / CloudKit: For optional data synchronization (subject to Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy)

No Other Third Parties

  • We do not use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) within the App
  • We do not use advertising networks
  • We do not integrate social media tracking
  • We do not share data with third-party marketing companies

Our website uses three third-party services, described in Website above: MailerLite (solely to operate the optional newsletter and email-course signups), Cloudflare Web Analytics (cookieless, aggregate page metrics), and Google Fonts (typeface delivery). None of them are used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete the App or manually delete quotes
  • iCloud Data: Retained in your iCloud account according to your iCloud settings and Apple's retention policies
  • Purchase History: Managed by Apple and retained according to Apple's policies
  • Newsletter and Course Signups: Kept in MailerLite until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your information — email feedback@quipsapp.com and we will remove you from our list

International Data Transfers

If you use the App outside your country of residence:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • If using iCloud, data may be transferred to iCloud servers in various regions
  • iCloud data transfers are managed by Apple according to their privacy policy

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you of significant changes through the App or App Store update notes
  • Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: We have described all data we collect in this policy
  • Right to Delete: You can delete all your app data by deleting the App and managing your iCloud data; for newsletter data, unsubscribe or email us and we will delete it
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate based on privacy choices

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent and for contract performance
  • Data Portability: You can export your data
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your app data yourself; for newsletter data, unsubscribe or email us and we will delete it
  • Data Controller: Tweeting Birds is the data controller for your App data and newsletter signups; Apple is the controller for iCloud data; MailerLite processes newsletter data on our behalf

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: feedback@quipsapp.com
Website: https://quipsapp.com

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
