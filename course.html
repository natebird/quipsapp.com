You're Losing the Best Words You'll Ever Hear

Think about it:

Last week, you probably read or heard something profound. Maybe it was:

  • Something your child said that made you laugh or cry
  • A line from a book that stopped you mid-page
  • Something your friend said that perfectly captured what you've been feeling
  • A podcast moment that shifted your perspective
  • Advice from a mentor that you swore you'd remember

But you didn't write it down.

And now it's gone.

Not because you didn't care. Because in the moment, you thought you'd remember. We always think we'll remember.

The truth is we remember the quotes we capture, and we lose the ones we don't.

This course will show you how to capture those fleeting moments before they're gone—building a collection of your kids' words that you'll treasure for decades.

Hi, I'm Nate

I've been collecting quotes for over a decade. Not because I'm particularly wise—I just wanted to avoid forgetting words that mattered.

What started as a long note on my iPhone turned into a collection of photos, screenshots, and eventually a digital journal with a complex tagging system. However, I could never find what I needed when I needed it.

So I created a system to capture quotes. And then I built an app (Quips) to make it easier to find and surface them.

This course shares everything I wish someone had taught me when I started. No theory. No fluff. Just the practical techniques that turned my collection into something genuinely useful.

You don't need an app to use these techniques (though if you want one, I built a good one). You just need 7 days and a willingness to pay attention.

Questions You Might Have

Is this really free?

Yes. Completely free. No ads. No credit card required. I built this to help people, not to sell them something on Day 3. (Though I do mention my app in the final email if you want a tool designed for quote collecting.)

How much time does it take?

10-20 minutes per day. Each email has one exercise. If you're short on time, you can read the email in 3 minutes and do the exercise later.

Do I need special tools or apps?

Nope. You can use whatever you already have—notes app, journal, voice memos. The course is platform-agnostic.

I'm not good at organizing. Will this work for me?

That's exactly who this is for. The system I teach is dead simple—5-7 categories max. No complicated filing systems.

What if I miss a day?

The emails stay in your inbox. You can catch up whenever. There's no deadline.

Can I use this for family quotes, not just famous ones?

Absolutely! In fact, many people find that capturing quotes from family members—especially kids—becomes the most valuable part of their collection. The course covers this extensively.

Can I unsubscribe?

Of course. There's an unsubscribe link at the bottom of every email. No hard feelings.

Will you sell my email address or spam me after the course?

Nope. After Day 7, you'll only get an occasional email (maybe once a month) with interesting quotes or tips. You can opt out of those too.

Do you teach how to find good quotes?

Not directly. This course is about capturing, organizing, and using quotes you encounter naturally. It's about developing the habit of paying attention.
